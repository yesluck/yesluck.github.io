About myself (关于我自己)


你好，我是林子坤。我现在是清华大学自动化系本科生，在今年秋季将成为哥伦比亚大学的计算机硕士。

Hi, I’m Zikun Lin, I’m now a bachelor student majoring in Automation (a.k.a. Computer Engineering in U.S.) in Tsinghua University, and I am an incoming master student in Columbia University’s computer science program from this fall.


在我的本科阶段，我培养起了对计算机科学的兴趣。在学习了一些相关课程后，我也参与了一些机器学习和自然语言处理的科研项目。我现在正在学习更多有关深度学习、算法和操作系统的相关知识。

In my undergraduate period, I cultivated my interest in computer science. After attending some related courses, I also conducted research in machine learning and natural language processing. And I’m now learning more about deep learning, algorithms and operating systems.


说到空余时间，我和系里、院里很多同学一样，喜欢看动画（这也是我在清华选了日语课的原因）。我也是osu!（一款不太热门的节奏游戏）的新手玩家。

As for my spare time, like many peers in my department and my school, I enjoy watching animations (and that’s why I’m taking Japanese courses in Tsinghua). I’m also an newbie in osu!, a not-so-well-known rhythm game.


如果你希望获得更多有关我的科研和项目经历的信息，请点击我的领英主页。

If you want to get more information about my research and project experience, please refer to my LinkedIn page.



About this website (关于这个网站)


这个网站将会包含我自2018年以来的学习内容的笔记和理解，包括Coursera课程“deeplearning.ai”、学堂在线课程“操作系统”、“Leetcode”中的算法分析和我正在清华上的日语课。

This website will contain my notes and understandings about my study from 2018, including Coursera course “deeplearning.ai”, Xuetangx course “Operating System”, algorithm analyses in “Leetcode” and the Japanese course I’m now taking in Tsinghua.


我也想在此分享我生活中的一些感受，如果我有时间的话……

I also want to share some feelings in my life here, if I have time…


我很抱歉我没法像这篇文章一样将每句话进行汉译英或英译汉。我更喜欢使用中文记录笔记，因为这能让我复习起来更方便。但是有些计算机名词不太方便翻译成中文，所以我文章的一些部分将会是英文（毕竟英语是国际语言……）

I’m sorry that I don’t have time to translate every sentence from Chinese to English or conversely, like this passage. I prefer to write my notes in Chinese since it’s much easier for me to review. But there are some terms in computer science which are difficult to translate to Chinese, so some parts of my passages will be in English (after all, English is the global language…)
